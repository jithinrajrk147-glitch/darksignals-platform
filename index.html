How DarkSignals Works

DarkSignals is a signal-driven advertising platform that connects advertisers with publishers. Our verification-first approach ensures transparency and trust. Advertisers create campaigns, publishers integrate ads, and our system verifies all activities in real-time.

Rules & Policies

  • All campaigns must comply with platform guidelines
  • Publishers must verify their content before earning
  • Minimum withdrawal amount: $50
  • Platform commission: 15% on all transactions
  • Abuse or fraud will result in immediate suspension
